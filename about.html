About Us

ENERZINX LLC

Founded in 2014 in California, USA, our firm specializes in expert engineering services focusing on electrical design, renewable energy project development, and comprehensive grid integration solutions. With a global presence and a dedicated team of 100 professionals spread across 5 locations in 4 countries, we offer deep expertise in mathematical modeling, power system analytics, grid code compliance, and wide-area system studies. As an employee-owned company, we take pride in our ability to deliver sustainable solutions that meet the highest standards of quality and efficiency. Since inception, we have successfully served over 140 clients worldwide, achieving profitability from day one and maintaining an impressive average revenue growth rate of 37% over the past 5 years. Our portfolio includes modeling over 125 GW of energy across more than 1100 projects in 15 countries, covering a vast network of 2 million miles of transmission lines. This extensive track record underscores our commitment to innovation and excellence, supporting global energy initiatives with reliable and environmentally conscious engineering solutions.

GSMT CONSULTING

GSMT Consulting is a leading provider of specialized electrical networks consulting services, dedicated to supporting Network Service Providers, regulators, and major Network Users. Our firm excels in strategic development and execution strategies, helping clients effectively manage and connect to electricity supply networks. Founded by seasoned professionals with extensive technical expertise in power system planning, modeling, and testing, GSMT focuses particularly on transmission networks. We work closely with network owners, managers, regulators, and major users to optimize network efficiency and drive economic and social development. Our approach integrates deep technical knowledge with strategic analysis, enabling us to deliver tailored solutions that meet our clients’ objectives effectively. GSMT is committed to providing unique and valuable resources to project and organizational teams, serving as trusted advisors in the energy sector. Our dedication to excellence ensures that we continually enhance network performance and support sustainable energy solutions worldwide.
