    • Registration and Compliance Assessment

    Overview

    Enerzinx and GSMT Consulting Joint Venture provides expert Registration and Compliance Assessment services tailored to ensure seamless integration and compliance for load and generation connections within the NER Rules. We specialize in interpreting and facilitating registration processes, negotiating NER Rule paths, and ensuring ongoing compliance with regulatory requirements. Our services include consideration of private network registrations and advising on obligations as NSP, load, or generation customers under NER. We conduct thorough testing procedures and pre-test simulations, offer onsite testing and provide necessary equipment for data collection. Our analysis includes Hold Point Reports and R2 Compliance and Model Validation Reports, ensuring adherence to AEMO, NSP, and OEM requirements. We coordinate onsite commissioning programs for all technologies, including solar, wind, battery storage, synchronous generators, synchronous condensers, SVCs, and STATCOMs, facilitating efficient and compliant project deployments.

    Services:

  • Interpretation and assistance with Load and Generation connections under NER Rules
  • Negotiation of NER Rule paths for registration and ongoing compliance
  • Consideration of private network registrations and obligations as NSP, Load, or Generation customer under NER
  • Testing procedures and pre-test simulations
  • Onsite testing, provision of test equipment, and data collection
  • Analysis including Hold Point Reports and R2 Compliance and Model Validation Report
  • Coordination of onsite commissioning programs
  • Engagement with AEMO, NSP, and OEM for compliance discussions
  • Expertise across all technologies including solar, wind, battery storage, synchronous generators, synchronous condensers, SVCs, STATCOMs, etc
  • Coordination of onsite commissioning programs
